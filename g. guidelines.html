TUTORING PACKAGES

    Please read these guidelines carefully before registering. They outline our expectations for conduct, punctuality, and payment to ensure a smooth and productive tutoring experience.

  • Students are expected to attend all scheduled sessions on time.
  • If you need to cancel or reschedule a session, please notify the tutor or administration at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Repeated late arrivals or absences without notice may result in suspension of tutoring services.

  • Sessions canceled with more than 24 hours’ notice will be rescheduled without penalty.
  • Sessions canceled with less than 24 hours’ notice may be or may not be charged in full.
  • Refunds will not be issued for unused or missed sessions unless approved by management.
  • If a tutor must cancel a session, a makeup session will be arranged at no extra cost.

  • Payment must be made before or on the first day of the scheduled sessions.
  • Accepted payment methods: bank transfer, mobile payment.
  • Monthly packages must be paid in advance.
  • Late payments up to one week may result in 50birr penalty per session; moreover late payments after the first two weeks without notice may result in suspension from the service.

  • Students are expected to behave respectfully toward tutors and other students.
  • Disruptive or inappropriate behavior may result in removal from the session.
  • Academic honesty is required at all times. Tutors will not complete assignments or exams on behalf of students.

  • For minors, parents/guardians are encouraged to monitor progress and communicate with the tutor regularly.
  • Progress reports can be provided upon request.





  • Students should come prepared with necessary materials (textbooks, notes, assignments).
  • For online sessions, students must ensure a stable internet connection and a quiet workspace.



  • All student information will be kept confidential and used only for educational purposes.
  • Tutors agree not to share personal or academic details without consent.




  • The tutoring center uses secure platform (Zoom, Google Meet, etc.) for online sessions.
  • Students and parents are responsible for familiarizing themselves with the platform in advance.




  • If technical problems interrupt a session, the tutor will attempt to reconnect.
  • If a session cannot proceed due to technical issues, it will be rescheduled.




  • The tutoring center reserves the right to terminate services for noncompliance with policies, nonpayment, or misconduct.
  • Any remaining balance after termination will be refunded at the center’s discretion.



  • The tutoring center provides a full packages without additional cost. All subjects listed on the form will be given on the online study sessions. Students are required to adjust themselves to the tutoring days and time.
  • The tutoring days and times might be chosen based on the majority students preference with regard to the tutors tutoring days.
